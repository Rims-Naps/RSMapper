[w][a][s][d] - Move camera forward/left/backward/right
Shift+[w][a][s][d] - Move selected tile forward/left/backward/right

[space] - Go up one plane
[pg up] - Go up one plane

[alt] - Go down one plane
[pg dwn] - Go down one plane

[tab] - Toggle load surrounding regions

[q] - Mouse-Select objects mode (select an object to lookup in object definitions by directly clicking on it)
