Prayer Guide
To begin Prayer kill NPCs around the world to obtain bones.
When you think you're strong enough to kill level 79 green dragons.
Pull the lever at home (Lever > East Dragons > Yes) and you will kill them
for dragon bones. Use all bones on the alter at home for maximum experience.
